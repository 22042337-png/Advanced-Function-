Parent function: f(x) = \( \dfrac{1}{x} \)

\( f(x) = \frac{-\frac{7}{3}x}{-\frac{3}{5}\left(x+\frac{50}{10}\right)} - \frac{7}{3} \)

Transformation:

The reason why I chose the reciprocal function because the bend of the reciprocal function is able to fit with the kink of the slide. This equation evolves the reciprocal function. The discrepancies from the parent function are:

  • Vertical stretch by a factor of \( \tfrac{7}{3} \)
  • Horizontal stretch by a factor of \( \tfrac{5}{3} \)
  • Horizontal translation toward the left for 5 units
  • Vertical translation downward for \( \tfrac{7}{3} \) units
  • No hole
  • Vertical asymptote at \( x = -5 \)
  • Horizontal asymptote at \( y = \tfrac{35}{9} \)

Domain and Range

The domain of this function is \( \{-2 \le x \le \tfrac{38}{10}\} \), the range of this function is \( \{-4.9 \le y \le -0.6\} \).
